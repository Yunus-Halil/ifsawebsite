The IFISA Model

Learning Built
for Real Futures

IFISA is a full school-embedded baccalaureate framework, not an enrichment programme. It runs alongside the national curriculum so students get both academic qualifications and future-ready STEM credentials.

Computational Thinking Across Disciplines

Algorithms, coding, and systems thinking woven across all subjects from age 5, not just computing class.

Portfolio-Based Credentials

Every student builds a 13-year Innovation Portfolio, their most powerful credential for university and beyond.

Human-Centered AI and Ethics

AI is taught as a societal force with responsibility, equity, and ethics integrated at every stage.

Real-World Problem Solving

Students apply STEM to genuine community challenges, not textbook exercises.

Curriculum Strands

Seven Strands.
Every Year. Every Stage.

01
Strand One

Computational Thinking and Coding

Algorithms, decomposition, pattern recognition, and programming applied across all subjects from age 5 through 18.

02
Strand Two

Mathematics for Modelling and Problem Solving

Applied mathematics, statistics, and quantitative reasoning used to solve real problems, not just pass exams.

03
Strand Three

Science and Inquiry

Empirical investigation, hypothesis testing, observation, data collection, and evidence-based reasoning across sciences.

04
Strand Four

Engineering, Robotics, and Making

Design-build-test-iterate cycles, mechanical and electronic systems, robotics, and digital fabrication.

05
Strand Five

Data, Statistics, and AI

Data literacy, visualisation, statistical analysis, AI concepts and algorithms, and AI ethics from early years through graduation.

06
Strand Six

Design, Creativity, and Communication

Visual communication, design thinking, creative problem solving, prototyping, and communicating technical ideas clearly.

07
Strand Seven

Ethics, Citizenship, and Leadership

Digital citizenship, AI ethics, environmental responsibility, equity, and leadership skills to drive change in complex systems.
