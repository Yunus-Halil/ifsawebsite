Adopt the Full IFISA Framework

Implement the complete 13-year pathway school-wide: from Early Years through Year 13: with full IFISA curriculum, assessment, and credential systems.

Implement Selected Stages or Pathways

Start with one or two developmental stages, or focus on the upper secondary specialisation pathways. IFISA is designed to scale gradually.

Co-Design Labs and Assessment Systems

Build Student Portfolio Systems

Implementation Roadmap

A Phased Path to Full Accreditation

IFISA implementation follows a structured three-phase journey: from initial pilot through to full accreditation and global network membership.

1

Phase 1: Pilot Launch (Years 1–2)

Curriculum pilot with selected year groups, initial teacher training, development of assessment rubrics, first IFISA student certificates awarded. School identity established as an IFISA Partner School.
