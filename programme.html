Programme Overview

Structured. Cumulative. Transformative.

The IFISA Programme is a 13-year developmental framework that runs alongside a school's national curriculum. It is designed to ensure that students build deep, cumulative competence over time rather than encountering STEM and technology in isolated bursts.

Every stage builds on the last. Students who complete the full programme graduate with not just knowledge, but with technical fluency, innovation portfolios, and the capacity to lead in a rapidly changing world.

Core Elements

Six Pillars of the IFISA Programme

Every stage of IFISA integrates these six pillars with increasing depth and complexity as learners progress.

Computational Thinking Across the Curriculum

Algorithms, decomposition, pattern recognition, and abstraction applied across all subjects: not just computing class.

Engineering and Design

Structured design-build-test-iterate cycles using real materials, tools, and engineering principles at every stage.

Data and AI Literacy

From basic data exploration to machine learning concepts: with ethics woven throughout.

Mathematical Modelling and Scientific Inquiry

Applied mathematics and empirical inquiry used to model real-world phenomena and test hypotheses.

Human-Centered Innovation

Design thinking, empathy, ethics, leadership, and community impact as explicit programme components.

Portfolio and Capstone Projects

Every student builds a visible portfolio across 13 years, culminating in a capstone project at each stage transition.

The IFISA Graduate

What a Completed IFISA Pathway Produces

Students who complete the full IFISA programme graduate with far more than academic qualifications.

Knowledge & Skills

  • Technical fluency in coding, data, and AI systems
  • Engineering design and prototyping experience
  • Mathematical modelling and quantitative reasoning
  • Scientific inquiry and evidence-based argumentation
  • Digital citizenship and AI ethics literacy

Credentials & Outputs

  • IFISA Innovation Portfolio spanning 13 years
  • Stage certificates and competency records
  • IFISA Diploma or Certificate (upper secondary)
  • Capstone project documentation and presentation
  • Industry exposure and university readiness record
